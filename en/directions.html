How do I get to Seoul BD Dental from Camp Humphreys or Seoul?

From Camp Humphreys: about 25–30 min by car (navigate to "서울비디치과" or 14 Buldang 34-gil, Cheonan). From Seoul: KTX to Cheonan-Asan (~35 min), then a 10-min taxi. Free underground parking on site; the clinic is open 365 days a year.

Routes

Choose Your Starting Point

🎖️ From Camp Humphreys

~30 min by car (26 km). Exit through the main gate → Route 45 south → follow signs to Cheonan/Buldang. GPS: "서울비디치과" or "14 Buldang 34-gil". Free parking in our basement — enter from the building side road.

🚄 From Seoul

KTX from Seoul Station to Cheonan-Asan Station: ~40 min (trains every 15–30 min). Then a 10-min taxi (~₩8,000) — show the driver: 천안 불당동 서울비디치과.

✈️ Just Arrived in Korea? (PCS / New Assignment)

Newly stationed at Camp Humphreys or relocating to the Cheonan–Asan area? From Incheon Airport: AREX to Seoul Station → KTX to Cheonan-Asan (~2 hrs), or airport limousine bus to Cheonan Terminal (~2.5 hrs). Book your first dental check-up as part of settling in — we keep English records for your PCM/insurance.

🚌 Local buses

Stop "Buldang Jugong 5-danji" (불당주공5단지) is a 1-minute walk from our door. Multiple city lines from Cheonan Station and Terminal.

🚉 Subway Line 1

Seoul Subway Line 1 runs to Cheonan Station (slower but cheapest from southern Seoul/Pyeongtaek). From Cheonan Station: 15 min by taxi.

🅿️ Parking

Free underground parking in our building. Height limit 2.1m. Street parking also available nearby.
