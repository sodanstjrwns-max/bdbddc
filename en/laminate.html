    FAQ

    Veneer FAQ

    How is Glownate different from regular veneers?

    Glownate is our in-house protocol combining ultra-minimal prep, custom ceramic layering and a proprietary bonding sequence — designed for a natural "glow" rather than the flat, opaque look of cheap veneers.

    Will my teeth be shaved down?

    Only 0.3–0.5mm on average — roughly the thickness of a fingernail. Suitable cases can be done with zero prep.

    How long do veneers last?

    With normal care, 10–15+ years. Our 10-year warranty program covers chips and debonding per its terms.

    Can I do it over a weekend?

    A Friday–Sunday schedule is often possible for 4–8 tooth cases. Contact us in advance so we can reserve the lab slot.

    I had veneers done elsewhere and hate them.

    Glownate Repair handles re-dos of failed or unnatural veneers from other clinics — pricing after assessment.

    Is Glownate available anywhere else?

    No. Glownate is Seoul BD Dental's proprietary premium-veneer technique, developed by Dr. Hyun Jung-min. It is performed only at our Cheonan clinic — patients fly in from Japan, the US and Southeast Asia specifically for it.

    Will my veneers look fake or too white?

    No — shade and translucency are custom-designed per patient using a digital smile design and natural ceramic layering. Our in-house lab technicians hand-finish every veneer, so the result glows like natural enamel rather than looking chalky.

    What warranty do I get?

    Glownate comes with a 10-year warranty program covering the veneer itself under normal use, plus free online aftercare follow-up for international patients. Full terms are explained in writing at your consult.

    Caring for Veneers

    Make Your Glownate Last 15 Years

    Avoid Extreme Force

    Don't bite fingernails, ice, or bottle caps with veneered teeth. Normal eating — including steak and apples — is completely fine.

    Night Guard if You Grind

    Bruxism is the #1 veneer killer. If you grind, a custom night guard (₩1M) protects your investment.

    Normal Brushing Works

    Brush and floss exactly like natural teeth. Non-abrasive toothpaste keeps the glaze glossy.

    Annual Check & Polish

    A yearly check-and-polish visit keeps margins sealed and the warranty program active.
