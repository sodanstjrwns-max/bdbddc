FAQ

How much does a dental implant cost at Seoul BD Dental?

Implant costs range from ₩800,000 to ₩1,500,000 per tooth depending on the fixture brand and bone condition. Foreigners pay the same price as Korean patients.

How long does implant treatment take?

Standard implants take 3-6 months total (2-3 visits). Same-day implants are available for eligible cases.

Is the surgery painful?

Modern implant surgery is minimally invasive. Local anesthesia is standard, and IV sedation is available for anxious patients.
